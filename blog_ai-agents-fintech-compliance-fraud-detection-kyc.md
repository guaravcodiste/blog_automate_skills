A fintech AI agent running on your compliance stack operates as a persistent process. Not a batch job. It holds state across your transaction ledger, KYC records, sanctions lists, and case management system simultaneously. Four agent types handle the full lifecycle.

Here are the four core agent types in a production agentic AI fintech compliance deployment:

These four agents run concurrently across every transaction and case in your pipeline. AI lifecycle management from transaction ingestion to SAR filing becomes the platform's job. That capacity shift is measurable in analyst hours and enforcement risk.
